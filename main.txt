TONG QUAN HE THONG SENSOR FUSION: LIDAR + CAMERA
==================================================

He thong gom 3 file chay tuan tu:
  1. collect_calibration.py  - Thu thap du lieu calibration
  2. compute_calibration.py  - Tinh toan ma tran chuyen doi
  3. fusion_calibration.py   - Chieu LiDAR len anh camera real-time

Luong hoat dong:
  collect (nhieu lan) -> compute (1 lan) -> fusion (chay lien tuc)


============================================================
FILE 1: collect_calibration.py
============================================================

MUC DICH:
  Thu thap cac cap diem tuong ung giua LiDAR va camera.
  Nguoi dung dat thanh trang truoc 2 cam bien, chon diem dau muc
  cua thanh tren du lieu LiDAR va tren anh camera.
  Ket qua: file JSON chua danh sach {goc, khoang_cach, pixel_x, pixel_y}.

COT LOI:
  - LiDAR chay tren thread rieng, doc du lieu lien tuc
  - Bieu do polar hien thi real-time, LUON SONG o moi tinh huong
  - Khi nhan "s": chup anh camera -> chon diem LiDAR -> chon diem anh -> noi suy tuyen tinh -> luu

CAC HAM CHINH:

  normalize_angle(angle)
    Chuyen goc tu dai 0-360 ve dai -180 den +180.
    Vi du: 350 do -> -10 do.

  filter_scan(scan_data)
    Loc du lieu LiDAR, chi giu cac diem trong pham vi +-90 do
    (trung voi truong nhin camera) va co khoang cach > 0.
    Sap xep theo goc tang dan.

  init_lidar()
    Ket noi LiDAR qua cong COM3, baudrate 115200.
    Kiem tra thong tin va tinh trang cam bien.

  init_realsense()
    Khoi tao camera D435: bat 2 luong depth (640x480, z16) va color (640x480, bgr8).
    Tao bo align de can chinh depth voi color.

  init_plot()
    Tao bieu do polar (bieu do tron) hien thi LiDAR real-time.
    Chi hien thi pham vi +-90 do, gioi han 6000mm.
    Dat cua so o vi tri (50, 50) tren man hinh.

  init_csv(file_name)
    Tao file CSV voi tieu de: Time, Angle, Distance.

  lidar_thread(lidar)
    Chay tren thread rieng (background).
    Doc lien tuc du lieu LiDAR, loc roi cap nhat vao bien latest_filtered.
    Dung scan_lock de dong bo voi thread chinh.

  get_scan()
    Lay ban sao du lieu scan moi nhat (an toan voi thread).

  update_polar(ax, line, fig, count)
    Cap nhat bieu do polar voi du lieu scan moi nhat.
    Goi draw_idle() va flush_events() de ve lai bieu do.

  save_csv(writer, scan, timestamp)
    Ghi du lieu scan vao file CSV.

  mouse_cb(event, x, y, flags, param)
    Ham callback xu ly click chuot tren anh camera.
    Click trai: them toa do vao danh sach, ve cham va ghi chu P1/P2.
    Toi da 2 diem.

  select_bar_points(scan_data, polar_ax, polar_line, polar_fig, count)
    Hien thi bieu do scatter (goc vs khoang cach) cua LiDAR.
    Nguoi dung click chon P1 va P2 la 2 dau muc cua thanh trang.
    Tim diem LiDAR gan nhat voi vi tri click bang khoang cach Euclidean chuan hoa.
    Sau khi chon xong + Enter: loc chi giu cac diem nam trong pham vi goc P1-P2.
    Trong luc cho, polar van cap nhat lien tuc.

  capture_frames(pipeline, align)
    Chup 1 cap anh (color + depth) tu camera.
    Align depth voi color de 2 anh khop pixel.

  select_image_points(color_img, polar_ax, polar_line, polar_fig, count)
    Hien thi anh RGB, cho nguoi dung click chon P1 va P2 tren thanh trang.
    Nhan Enter de xac nhan, 'q' de huy.
    Polar van cap nhat trong luc cho.

  map_lidar_to_image(bar_points, p1, p2)
    Anh xa cac diem LiDAR thuoc thanh sang toa do pixel.
    Dung noi suy tuyen tinh:
      t = (goc - goc_min) / (goc_max - goc_min)
      pixel_x = P1_x + t * (P2_x - P1_x)
      pixel_y = P1_y + t * (P2_y - P1_y)
    Diem goc nho nhat -> P1, diem goc lon nhat -> P2.

  show_result(color_img, mapped_points, p1, p2, ...)
    Hien thi anh ket qua voi duong noi P1-P2 va cac cham xanh la
    tai vi tri diem LiDAR da mapping.
    Nhan Enter de tiep tuc. Polar van song.

  save_all(color_img, depth_img, result_img, calibration_data, timestamp)
    Luu toan bo vao thu muc captured_data/:
      - color/: anh mau goc
      - depth/: anh do sau goc (16-bit)
      - depth_colormap/: anh do sau dang mau (de nhin)
      - calibration/: anh ket qua + file JSON

  calibrate(pipeline, align, scan_data, timestamp, ...)
    Dieu phoi toan bo quy trinh 1 lan calibration:
      1. Chup anh camera truoc (tranh timeout)
      2. Mo scatter LiDAR, chon P1 P2 cua thanh
      3. Mo anh RGB, chon P1 P2 tren anh
      4. Noi suy mapping LiDAR -> pixel
      5. Hien thi ket qua
      6. Luu tat ca

  main_loop(lidar, writer, pipeline, align)
    Vong lap chinh:
      - Khoi tao bieu do polar
      - Bat thread LiDAR chay background
      - Lien tuc cap nhat polar moi 0.2 giay
      - Khi nhan 's': chay calibrate()
      - Ctrl+C de thoat

  cleanup(lidar, csv_file, pipeline)
    Tat thread LiDAR, dong file CSV, ngat ket noi LiDAR,
    dong tat ca cua so, dung camera.


============================================================
FILE 2: compute_calibration.py
============================================================

MUC DICH:
  Doc tat ca file JSON calibration da thu thap, tinh toan ma tran
  chuyen doi tu toa do LiDAR sang toa do pixel camera.
  Su dung 3 phuong phap, so sanh va chon phuong phap tot nhat.
  Ket qua: file calibration_result.npz.

COT LOI:
  - Chuyen LiDAR tu toa do cuc (goc, khoang cach) sang Descartes (x, z)
  - Tim ma tran bien doi bang 3 thuat toan khac nhau
  - Danh gia bang sai so tai chieu (reprojection error)
  - Tu dong chon phuong phap co sai so thap nhat

CAC HAM CHINH:

  load_calibration_jsons(calibration_dir)
    Doc tat ca file calibration_*.json trong thu muc.
    Voi moi diem: chuyen tu toa do cuc sang Descartes:
      x = distance * sin(angle)    (thanh phan ngang)
      z = distance * cos(angle)    (thanh phan sau, huong phia truoc)
    Ket qua: 2 mang numpy:
      lidar_pts: moi dong la [x, z] (don vi mm)
      pixel_pts: moi dong la [pixel_x, pixel_y]

  get_realsense_intrinsics()
    Lay thong so noi tai (intrinsics) cua camera D435:
      - fx, fy: tieu cu (focal length)
      - cx, cy: tam quang hoc (principal point)
      - dist_coeffs: he so meo ong kinh
    Neu khong ket noi duoc camera, dung gia tri mac dinh.
    Ma tran K (3x3):
      | fx  0  cx |
      | 0  fy  cy |
      | 0   0   1 |

  method_homography(lidar_pts, pixel_pts)
    Phuong phap 1: Tim ma tran Homography H (3x3).
    Cong thuc: [u, v, 1]^T = H * [x, z, 1]^T (rồi chia cho w)
    Dung cv2.findHomography() voi RANSAC (loai diem nhieu).
    Can toi thieu 4 cap diem.
    Uu diem: don gian, linh hoat (co perspective).
    Nhuoc diem: khong tach rieng duoc R, T.

  method_solvepnp(lidar_pts, pixel_pts, K, dist_coeffs)
    Phuong phap 2: Tim R (xoay) va T (dich chuyen) giua 2 cam bien.
    Mo rong diem LiDAR 2D thanh 3D: [x, 0, z] (y=0 vi LiDAR quet ngang).
    Dung cv2.solvePnP() ket hop voi camera intrinsics K.
    Thu 2 bien the: ITERATIVE va SQPNP, chon cai sai so thap hon.
    Uu diem: tach rieng duoc R, T, dung duoc intrinsics.
    Nhuoc diem: tat ca diem dong phang (y=0) nen khong on dinh.

  method_affine(lidar_pts, pixel_pts)
    Phuong phap 3: Tim ma tran Affine A (2x3).
    Cong thuc: [u, v]^T = A * [x, z, 1]^T
    Dung cv2.estimateAffine2D() voi RANSAC.
    Can toi thieu 3 cap diem.
    Uu diem: on dinh voi it diem, khong bi overfitting.
    Nhuoc diem: khong mo hinh hoa perspective.

  visualize_results(lidar_pts, pixel_pts, H, A, calibration_dir)
    Hien thi anh so sanh ket qua:
      - Cham xanh la: ground truth (pixel that)
      - Cham do: ket qua Homography
      - Cham vang: ket qua Affine
    Cac cham cang gan nhau = calibration cang tot.

  main()
    Dieu phoi toan bo:
      1. Load tat ca file JSON
      2. Lay camera intrinsics
      3. Chay 3 phuong phap
      4. So sanh sai so, chon phuong phap tot nhat
      5. Luu vao calibration_result.npz
      6. Hien thi anh so sanh


============================================================
FILE 3: fusion_calibration.py
============================================================

MUC DICH:
  Chay real-time, chieu cac diem LiDAR len anh camera lien tuc
  bang ma tran calibration da tinh. Moi frame camera duoc overlay
  cac cham mau tuong ung voi vi tri LiDAR.

COT LOI:
  - Load ma tran tu file .npz (chon phuong phap tot nhat)
  - LiDAR chay tren thread rieng
  - Moi frame: chuyen tung diem LiDAR (goc, khoang cach) -> pixel (u, v) -> ve cham mau
  - Mau cham theo khoang cach: xanh la (gan) -> vang -> do (xa)

CAC HAM CHINH:

  normalize_angle(angle)
    Giong file collect: chuyen goc 0-360 ve -180 den +180.

  load_calibration()
    Doc file calibration_result.npz.
    Lay ten phuong phap tot nhat (homography hoac affine).
    Tra ve (ten phuong phap, ma tran tuong ung).
    Neu khong co file: thoat chuong trinh.

  lidar_to_pixel(angle_deg, distance_mm, method, matrix)
    Chuyen 1 diem LiDAR sang toa do pixel:
      1. Chuyen goc sang radian
      2. Tinh x = distance * sin(angle), z = distance * cos(angle)
      3. Neu Homography: pixel = H * [x, z, 1], chia cho w
         Neu Affine: pixel = A * [x, z, 1]
    Tra ve (u, v) la toa do pixel tren anh.

  dist_to_color(dist_mm, min_d=300, max_d=4000)
    Tinh mau cho diem dua theo khoang cach:
      - Gan (< 2150mm): xanh la dan sang vang
      - Xa (> 2150mm): vang dan sang do
    Tra ve (B, G, R) theo dinh dang BGR cua OpenCV.

  lidar_thread()
    Thread rieng chay LiDAR lien tuc.
    Ket noi LiDAR ngay trong thread.
    Loc +-90 do, cap nhat latest_scan.

  init_realsense()
    Giong file collect: khoi tao camera D435.

  main()
    Vong lap chinh:
      1. Load ma tran calibration
      2. Bat thread LiDAR
      3. Khoi tao camera
      4. Moi frame:
         - Lay anh color tu camera
         - Lay scan LiDAR moi nhat
         - Voi moi diem LiDAR: chuyen sang pixel, kiem tra bounds,
           ve cham mau len anh
         - Hien thi so diem, phuong phap dang dung
         - Nhan 'q' de thoat


============================================================
TOM TAT SO SANH 3 PHUONG PHAP CALIBRATION
============================================================

                  Homography      solvePnP        Affine
Ma tran           H (3x3)         R (3x3) + T     A (2x3)
Dau vao LiDAR     2D (x, z)       3D (x, 0, z)    2D (x, z)
Dau vao camera    2D (u, v)       2D (u, v)        2D (u, v)
Can intrinsics    Khong           Co (K)           Khong
So diem toi thieu 4               4                3
Co perspective    Co              Co               Khong
Do on dinh        Trung binh      Thap*            Cao
RANSAC            Co              Khong            Co

* solvePnP khong on dinh vi tat ca diem dong phang (y=0)
